Microphone Test & Permission Fix

Click "Test Microphone" to check if your microphone is working.

Troubleshooting Steps:

  1. Allow Microphone Permission: When prompted, click "Allow" to grant microphone access
  2. Check Browser URL Bar: Look for a microphone icon in the address bar and ensure it's not blocked
  3. Reset Permissions: Go to browser settings and reset site permissions for localhost
  4. Try Different Browser: Test with Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
  5. Check System Audio: Ensure your microphone is not muted in system settings

Browser Permission Reset Instructions:

Chrome:

  • Click the lock/microphone icon in the address bar
  • Set microphone to "Allow"
  • Or go to chrome://settings/content/microphone and manage permissions
